How to Play Simon
The objective of the game is to remember the pattern as far as you can. In the start, a color will flash. Press that color to repeat the sequence. The unit will add another color to the sequence which you then repeat by pressing in the correct order. The unit will continue to add colors after you successfully repeat the sequence back.
